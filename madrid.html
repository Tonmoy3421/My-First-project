Let's know about Madrid

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol is a professional football club based in Madrid,Spain.
It is one of the most well-known teams in the world.
  • Founded in 1902 as Madrid Football Club
  • Joined the Royal Spanish Football Federation in 1909
  • King Alfonso XIII gave the club the name "Real" in 1920
  • Plays home games at the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium

Achievements of Madrid

  1. Won the European Cup a record 15 times
  2. One of the founding members of La Liga
  3. Has never been relegated from the top division
  4. Has won the Copa del Rey multiple times
  5. Has won the regional championship multiple times
